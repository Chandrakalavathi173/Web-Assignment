ABOUT

Good Shepherd English Medium School was started in the year 1989 in Nandyal of Kurnool district, Andhra Pradesh,under the private managementof Mr.L.T.Ratna Paul as the Director and Mrs.L.T.Jessy Paul as the Principal.It began with 75 students studying Nursery to III classes supported by three teachers. Driven by honesty, hard work and service, the school quickly grew in strength and stature. Today it serves 6000 students through 380 staff and is recognized as one of the few schools in the state known for high academic standards.
