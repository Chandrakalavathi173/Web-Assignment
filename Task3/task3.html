Good Shepherd E.M School, Nandyal has been one of the pioneers in educational sector of A.P over a period of 29 years. (Established in 1989), Good Shepherd E.M School strives to create responsible modern global citizens with core values of competence, Character and Contribution. We provide our children with state - of - the - art Educational Infrastructure with technology enabled education. Ours is a State Board Curriculum with 6000 students and 380 well qualified and most experienced teachers. At Good Shepherd, we believe that a dream we dream together becomes reality. ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE SINCE 1989

The school has excelled in Science fairs, Karate, Chess and Art at State and National level. It's students have been receiving Prathibha Awards from Government of Andhra Pradesh in academics and Star of India and Star of Andhra awards and SAAP scholarships in various sports and games consistently.It has to its credit hundreds of state players and around 100 National players in various disciplines.
