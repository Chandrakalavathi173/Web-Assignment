Good Shepherd E.M School, Nandyal has been one of the pioneers in educational sector of A.P over a period of 29 years. (Established in 1989), Good Shepherd E.M School strives to create responsible modern global citizens with core values of competence, Character and Contribution. We provide our children with state - of - the - art Educational Infrastructure with technology enabled education. Ours is a State Board Curriculum with 6000 students and 380 well qualified and most experienced teachers. At Good Shepherd, we believe that a dream we dream together becomes reality.

Driven by honesty, hard work and service, the school quickly grew in strength and stature. Today it serves 6000 students through 380 staff and is recognized as one of the few schools in the state known for high academic standards.

The school has many long standing staff whose untiring efforts keep it in its successful orbit. The school has a consistent output of 100% results in S.S.C exams in its last 21 batches. The school has become a hub for sports and games and has organized state level and national level competitions through recognized bodies of Cricket, Handball and Ball badminton.

The school has excelled in Science fairs, Karate, Chess and Art at State and National level. It’s students have been receiving Prathibha Awards from Government of Andhra Pradesh in academics and Star of India and Star of Andhra awards and SAAP scholarships in various sports and games consistently.It has to its credit hundreds of state players and around 100 National players in various disciplines.

The school has consciously designed a holistic approach to education and implements it through various school committees. This aims at moulding a child into multifaceted personality by providing multiple opportunities to draw out their diverse innate talents.

The school's vision and mission is to ultimately build future citizens with a high balance of competence and character towards a life of high contribution. Children are one third of our population, but all of our future.
